# Concurrence Log -- Datastream Books

> Decisions made by the Technical Strategic Lead under [§71](./datastream-books-decisions.md) authority that affect stakeholders beyond the technical domain. Each entry has a 5-business-day window for stakeholders to raise concerns; after that window, the decision is locked. Concerns raised in-window are addressed and resolutions captured here.
>
> This log is separate from the formal decision log ([`./datastream-books-decisions.md`](./datastream-books-decisions.md)); the formal log captures decisions, this log captures the stakeholder visibility and concurrence process around cross-domain decisions.

## How this works

- Technical Strategic Lead makes a decision under [§71](./datastream-books-decisions.md) authority
- Decision is logged formally in the decision log with a § number
- A row is added to this concurrence log within 24 hours of the decision
- Notification is sent to stakeholders in the "Stakeholders notified" column (channel: email summary, in-person, or via weekly status update -- captured in "Notification method")
- Concern window opens; stakeholders have 5 business days to raise concerns
- Concerns raised are captured in "Concerns raised" and resolved with the Technical Strategic Lead; resolution captured in "Resolution"
- After window closes with no concerns OR all concerns resolved, "Status" updates to "Locked"
- If a concern fundamentally changes the decision, a new formal decision-log entry is created and a new row is added here referencing the supersession
